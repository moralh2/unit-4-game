Crystal Collector

Hello, traveller. The game is simple. You are given a random number at the start of the game. Your aim is to match your total score to that random number by clicking on the crystals!

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on one, you add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number; you lose the game if you go above it. The value of each crystal is hidden from you up until you clik on it. Each time the game starts, the values associated with each crystal change.
